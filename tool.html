Toolkit Builder Beta

It's easy to get started on Toolkit Builder.
Now that you have completed the setup on Diligent One platform, you can easily prepare and list the Toolkit in your Marketplace using the Toolkit Builder.

Step 1: Prepare your Toolkit to test

Select the Toolkit elements to be packed and listed in test organizations.

Step 2: Test your Toolkit

Review the modules and activate the Toolkit in test environments. Capture test results in Toolkit Builder.

Step 3: Submit for Diligent's review

Submit the Toolkit and test results for Diligent's review from Toolkit Builder.

Step 4: List Toolkit on Marketplace

Publish your Toolkit on Marketplace if approved to streamline workflow solutions across various organizations.
